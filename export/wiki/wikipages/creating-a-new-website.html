Creating a new website

Select 
site overview
at the right top corner selection box.
A page appears that shows an overwiew over all your existing websites, and a button to create a new one. Click this button.
In the form displayed, fill in the parameters for your new site. 
Akismet
and 
Defensio
are Anti Spam tools (to prevent any unknown users to write anything unwanted about your contributions). Your basic site is ready to be modified for your purpose.
In case you already have sites listed in your site overview: before you create a new site, you need to change the number of your localhost for your exiting site(s) 

http://localhost:3000
to 

http://localhost:3xxx (another number)
or you have to start an new local server for the new site. Thus this site starts completely from scratch with the default templates.
